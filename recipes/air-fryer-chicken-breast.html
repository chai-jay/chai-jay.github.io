Air Fryer Chicken Breast

Ingredients

  • 3lb chicken breast
  • Oil

  • Marinade:

    • 2 tbsp garlic salt
    • 1.5 tbsp cornstarch
    • 3 tsp sugar
    • 0.5 tsp white pepper
    • 1.5 tbsp oyster sauce
    • 1 tbsp fish sauce
    • 2 tsp light soy sauce
    • 3 tsp Shaoxing cooking wine

Steps

  1. Rinse chicken breasts in water and pat dry with paper towerls.
  2. Cut into thickest part of each chicken breast to try to get even thickness for even cooking.
  3. In a bowl, combine marinade ingredients and mix well.
  4. Add chicken breasts to the marinade. Massage/squeeze the chicken to work the marinade into the meat. Then let it marinade for at least 30 minutes.
  5. Place breasts in air fryer. Spread oil (or spray oil spray) on top surface for a nice crust.
  6. Air fry for 20 minutes at 380 degrees F. Flip in between.
